Battle-tested frameworks for finding, validating, and scaling profitable ecommerce products.

## 3. The Winning Product Checklist

Score each criterion 1-5. Minimum 35/50 to proceed.

| Criterion | Score | Notes |
|:----------|:------|:------|
| Scroll-stop visual appeal | /5 | Would this stop a TikTok scroll? |
| Problem/desire strength | /5 | How badly do they want/need this? |
| Impulse buy price point ($15-50) | /5 | Can they buy without thinking? |
| Margin potential (3x+ markup) | /5 | Room for profitable ads? |
| Shipping friendliness | /5 | Small, light, not fragile? |
| Content/demo potential | /5 | Easy to show value in video? |
| Not easily found locally | /5 | Can't grab it at Target? |
| Low return risk | /5 | Not sizing-dependent, not fragile? |
| Repeat purchase / upsell potential | /5 | Will they buy again or buy more? |
| Trend alignment (not fad) | /5 | Growing or stable demand? |
| **Total** | **/50** | |

- 40-50: Strong winner — move fast
- 35-39: Promising — test it
- 25-34: Weak — needs a stronger angle or pivot
- Below 25: Kill it

## 4. Product Page Conversion Blueprint

```
ABOVE THE FOLD:
├── Hero image/video (lifestyle, not white background)
├── Product title (benefit-driven, not feature-driven)
├── Price + compare-at price (show savings)
├── Star rating + review count
├── 3 bullet benefit icons
├── Add to Cart button (sticky on mobile)
└── Trust badges (money-back, fast shipping, secure checkout)

BELOW THE FOLD:
├── GIF or video demo showing product in action
├── "Why [Product Name]?" — 3-4 benefit blocks with icons
├── Social proof section (UGC photos, testimonial quotes)
├── Before/After or comparison section
├── "What's Included" — bundle/value stack visual
├── FAQ section (address top 5 objections)
├── Final CTA with urgency element
└── "You May Also Like" — cross-sell section
```

## 5. Ad Creative Framework

### The 5 Video Ad Hooks That Work

1. **Problem-Agitate** — "Tired of [problem]? There's finally a fix."
2. **Transformation** — Show the before/after in the first 2 seconds
3. **Unboxing/Reveal** — "Wait until you see what's inside..."
4. **Social Proof** — "Over 50,000 sold and here's why..."
5. **POV/Lifestyle** — "POV: You finally found [product] and it changed everything"

### Ad Structure (15-30 seconds)

```
0-3s:  HOOK — Stop the scroll (problem, question, or visual wow)
3-10s: DEMO — Show the product solving the problem
10-20s: PROOF — Testimonial, results, or social proof
20-30s: OFFER — Price, bundle, guarantee, CTA
```

### Creative Testing Protocol

- Test 3-5 different hooks with the same product
- Spend $20-50 per creative before killing
- Winning metrics: CTR > 1.5%, Hook rate > 30% (TikTok)
- Scale winners, kill losers, iterate on middle performers

## 6. Pricing Psychology for Ecom

### Price Anchoring Tactics

| Tactic | Example | Why It Works |
|:-------|:--------|:------------|
| Compare-at price | ~~$59.99~~ $34.99 | Perceived savings trigger |
| Bundle anchor | "Buy 1 for $29 or 3 for $59" | Makes bundle feel like a steal |
| Cost-of-problem anchor | "Stop wasting $200/yr on [inferior solution]" | Reframes price as investment |
| Per-day framing | "Less than $1/day" | Makes any price seem trivial |
| Value stack | List everything included with individual prices | Total value > price paid |

### Optimal Price Points (impulse-buy ecom)

```
Sweet spot 1: $19.99-24.99  — Pure impulse, lowest friction
Sweet spot 2: $29.99-39.99  — Impulse with slight consideration
Sweet spot 3: $49.99-59.99  — Needs strong offer/proof
Above $60:    Needs brand trust, reviews, or payment plans (Afterpay, Klarna)
```

## 7. Email & SMS Revenue Machine

### The 5 Essential Flows (set up before spending on ads)

| Flow | Trigger | Expected Revenue |
|:-----|:--------|:----------------|
| Welcome Series (3-5 emails) | Email signup | 5-10% of total revenue |
| Abandoned Cart (3 emails + 1 SMS) | Cart abandonment | 5-15% of total revenue |
| Post-Purchase (3 emails) | Order confirmed | Drives reviews + repeat purchase |
| Browse Abandonment (2 emails) | Viewed product, didn't add to cart | 2-5% recovery |
| Win-Back (3 emails) | 60-90 days since last purchase | Re-engages 5-10% of churned |

**Email/SMS should generate 25-40% of total revenue for a mature ecom brand.**

## 8. Scaling Decision Framework

```
Phase 1: Testing ($50-200/day ad spend)
├── Goal: Find winning product + creative
├── Timeline: 1-2 weeks
├── Key metric: ROAS > break-even
└── Decision: Kill or scale

Phase 2: Validation ($200-500/day)
├── Goal: Confirm profitability at higher spend
├── Timeline: 2-4 weeks
├── Key metric: Consistent ROAS > 2x, stable CPAs
└── Decision: Scale aggressively or optimize

Phase 3: Scaling ($500-2000+/day)
├── Goal: Maximize revenue while maintaining margins
├── Timeline: Ongoing
├── Key metric: Net profit margin > 20%
├── Actions: Add channels, launch lookalikes, expand geo
└── Decision: When to launch next product

Phase 4: Brand Building
├── Goal: Reduce reliance on paid ads
├── Actions: SEO, organic social, PR, influencer program
├── Key metric: Organic revenue > 30% of total
└── Moat: Brand recognition, repeat customers, product line
```

## 11. Key Ecom Metrics Cheat Sheet

| Metric | Target | What It Tells You |
|:-------|:-------|:-----------------|
| CTR (ad click-through) | > 1.5% | Is the ad creative working? |
| CPC (cost per click) | < $2.00 | Is traffic affordable? |
| ATC Rate (add to cart) | > 5% | Is the product page converting? |
| CVR (purchase conversion) | > 1.5% | Is the offer compelling? |
| AOV (average order value) | > $40 | Are upsells/bundles working? |
| ROAS (return on ad spend) | > 2.5x | Are you profitable on ads? |
| CAC (customer acquisition cost) | < 30% of AOV | Can you afford to acquire? |
| LTV:CAC ratio | > 3:1 | Is the business sustainable? |
| Return rate | < 10% | Is product quality acceptable? |
| Email revenue % | 25-40% | Are you capturing repeat value? |
