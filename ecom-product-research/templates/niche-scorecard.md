# Ecom Niche Scorecard

Use this template to evaluate and compare ecommerce niches before committing to a product.

## 1. Niche Evaluation Matrix

Score each criterion 1-5 for each niche under consideration:

| Criterion | Niche A | Niche B | Niche C | Why It Matters |
|:----------|:--------|:--------|:--------|:---------------|
| Market size & demand | /5 | /5 | /5 | Bigger = more customers |
| Trend direction (growing?) | /5 | /5 | /5 | Rising tide lifts all boats |
| Passion level of buyers | /5 | /5 | /5 | Passionate buyers spend more, share more |
| Willingness to pay premium | /5 | /5 | /5 | Margins depend on this |
| Content/UGC potential | /5 | /5 | /5 | Can customers create content with your product? |
| Competition level (moderate = good) | /5 | /5 | /5 | Too low = no demand, too high = can't compete |
| Repeat purchase potential | /5 | /5 | /5 | LTV is everything |
| Product line expansion | /5 | /5 | /5 | Can you sell 5-10 products to this customer? |
| Influencer ecosystem | /5 | /5 | /5 | Are there creators in this niche? |
| Seasonality (year-round = 5) | /5 | /5 | /5 | Consistent revenue > seasonal spikes |
| **Total** | **/50** | **/50** | **/50** | |

### Scoring Guide
- 40-50: Excellent niche — move fast
- 30-39: Good niche — worth testing
- 20-29: Mediocre — needs a stronger angle
- Below 20: Avoid

**Key questions:**
- Are the top 3 competitors doing > $100K/month? (proves demand)
- Are there gaps in their product line? (your entry point)
- What do their 1-star reviews say? (your product improvement)
- How professional is their branding? (can you out-brand them?)
- Are they running ads? (if yes, the market supports paid acquisition)

## 5. Niche Expansion Map

Start narrow, expand over time:

```
CORE PRODUCT (your entry)
    |
    v
COMPLEMENTARY PRODUCTS (cross-sell)
    |
    v
CONSUMABLES/REFILLS (recurring revenue)
    |
    v
PREMIUM LINE (higher AOV)
    |
    v
ADJACENT NICHE (new customer segment)
```

Example:
```
Core: Dog hiking harness
  -> Complementary: Collapsible water bowl, poop bag holder
  -> Consumable: Dog treats for hikes, joint supplements
  -> Premium: GPS tracker collar, insulated dog jacket
  -> Adjacent: Cat outdoor harness, horse trail gear
```
