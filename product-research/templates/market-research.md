# Market Research Deep Dive Template

Use this template when Phase 2 (Market Gap Analysis) needs deeper investigation.

## 1. Total Addressable Market (TAM) Estimation

| Layer | Description | Estimate |
|:------|:------------|:---------|
| TAM | Everyone who could theoretically use this | $ |
| SAM | Segment you can realistically reach | $ |
| SOM | What you can capture in Year 1 | $ |

For Micro SaaS, focus on SOM. You don't need a billion-dollar TAM — you need 500 paying users at $19/mo = $9,500/mo.

## 5. Google Trends Interpretation Guide

| Pattern | What it means | Action |
|:--------|:-------------|:-------|
| Flat & steady | Consistent demand, reliable market | Green light |
| Rising steadily | Growing market, get in early | Strong green light |
| Spike then crash | Hype cycle, not sustainable | Caution |
| Declining | Shrinking market | Avoid unless you see a new angle |
| Seasonal peaks | Predictable demand windows | Plan launches around peaks |

## 6. Willingness-to-Pay Indicators

Strong buying signals:
- People already paying for inferior solutions
- Businesses losing money due to this problem (compliance fines, chargebacks, errors)
- Time cost is measurable (X hours/week wasted)
- The user has a job title (they have budget authority)
- Industry has high average revenue per employee

Weak buying signals:
- "That would be nice to have"
- Target audience is students, hobbyists, or early-stage solopreneurs
- Problem is annoying but not costly
- Existing free tools are "good enough"
- No one is searching for a paid solution
